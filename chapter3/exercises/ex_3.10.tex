\begin{Exercise}
\begin{proof}
By hypothesis, we know $a_n\in\mathbb{Z}$ and $|a_n|\geq 1$. Since
$$
\alpha := \limsup_{n\to\infty} \sqrt[n]{|a_n|}
\geq \limsup_{n\to\infty} \sqrt[n]{1}
= 1,
$$
then
$$
R = \frac{1}{\alpha}
\leq 1.
$$
This means the radius of convergence is at most $1$.
\end{proof}
\end{Exercise}
